Welcome to MysticB & B School

Where Passion Meets Professional Excellence

At MysticB & B School, we believe in transforming passion into profession. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, we are Nepal's premier training center for aspiring baristas, bartenders, and culinary professionals.

With years of industry experience and certified trainers, we provide comprehensive training programs that combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical sessions. Our state-of-the-art facilities and industry-standard equipment ensure that every student receives world-class education.
