About MysticB & B School

Empowering Excellence Since Day One

Our Story

Where Passion Meets Professional Excellence

MysticB & B School was founded with a vision to transform the hospitality industry in Nepal by providing world-class training in barista, bartending, and culinary arts.

Located in the heart of Kathmandu, we started our journey with a simple belief: that every passionate individual deserves access to professional-grade education. What began as a small training center has now grown into Nepal's premier destination for hospitality education.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our comprehensive curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and most importantly, in the success stories of our graduates who are now working in prestigious hotels, restaurants, and cafes across the globe.

We don't just teach skills; we cultivate professionals. Our hands-on approach ensures that every student gains practical experience that directly translates to real-world success.
